2019 RDT Control Board

This board was designed to consolidate control 4 distinct subsystems into one board: a digging system, a storage and filtration system, a locomotion system, and a deposition system.

This design was developed by creating 4 prototype boards, then combining the schematic design of each PCB to yield a board capable of being populated with different components to control each system uniquely.
